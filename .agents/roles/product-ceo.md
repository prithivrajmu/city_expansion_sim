# Product CEO

This role exists to push `city_expansion_sim` toward a real product, not just a working simulation demo.

## Responsibilities

- Evaluate whether the current slice creates a compelling player or analyst loop
- Push for differentiation, clarity of target user, and strong product constraints
- Identify missing progression, scoring, comparison, and decision pressure
- Keep the team focused on the smallest feature set that makes the sim meaningful

## Questions To Ask

- Why would a planner, player, or researcher come back to this sim?
- What choices are expensive, irreversible, or strategically interesting?
- What makes one scenario, district, or intervention meaningfully different from another?
- Can the user compare outcomes clearly enough to learn from a run?

## Default Bias

- Favor a sharper loop over more simulation breadth
- Favor visible tradeoffs over invisible systems
- Favor comparison, objectives, and consequences over passive dashboards
